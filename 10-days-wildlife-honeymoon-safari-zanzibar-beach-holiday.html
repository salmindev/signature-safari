Honeymoon Safari & Zanzibar

Description

08 Days Wildlife Honeymoon Safari & Zanzibar

Tanzania honeymoon packages are designed with all needed ingredients for safari & Zanzibar beach holidays to make this special event for newly married couples a life time experience & a memorable trip in Africa. At Signature Safari we have many options for such an important event but we always invite you to go through all options & we lastly compile all thoughts & ideas to design a honeymoon package of your dreams.Serengeti Wildebeest Migration, The big 5 & the big cats is what you may expect to experience during the safari before enjoying the white sand beaches of Zanzibar islands.

DAY 1: ARRIVAL – ARUSHA / SAFARI BRIEFING

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be met by your driver and transferred to the accommodation in Arusha town for hotel relaxation from jet-lag. You will also be given safari briefing on this day so that you can be ready for the next day wildlife safari. Dinner and overnight will be served at the accommodation at Arusha Gran Melia an opening out onto all of Tanzania’s majestic natural beauty. An oasis of five-star comfort and elegance, with extensive, unbeatable facilities, exclusive suites, and international cuisine, including the outstanding The Grill restaurant. With a spa, heated pool, and gym.

Accommodation: Arusha Gran Melia Hotel

DAY 2: ARUSHA TOWN TOUR

This day of your Wildlife Honeymoon Safari will be very relaxed day, waking up at your hotel in Arusha Gran Melia , After breakfast, around 9:00AM at the Hotel in Arusha Town and then derive to Arusha City Tour-will visit Arusha Natural Museum (Boma Road), Clock Tower, or (EAC –East Africa Head office), Arusha International Conference Hall( AICC) Maasai markert, Arusha central market(Soku Kuu!), Sokoine Road, Shoppers Mall, later sample local/African’s dishes/ hot lunch around town.Dinner and overnight will be served at the accommodation at Arusha Gran Melia an opening out onto all of Tanzania’s majestic natural beauty. An oasis of five-star comfort and elegance, with extensive, unbeatable facilities, exclusive suites, and international cuisine, including the outstanding The Grill restaurant. With a spa, heated pool, and gym.

Accommodation: Arusha Gran Melia Hotel

DAY 2: TARANGIRE NATIONAL PARK

The day will begin with a yummy and healthy breakfast. As explained our driver will pick you up and drop you at the first adventure spot in Tarangire National Park. At the Tarangire National Park, you will get the opportunity to game drive i.e. view all the animals in their natural habitat. In the game drive session, you will not only get to see wildebeest but also zebras, elephant, Maasai giraffe, Baboons, Fringe-eared Oryx. There’s also a high possibility that you will spot predators like Cheetah, Lions, leopard or the rarest of the rare tree-climbing python. Moreover, you will come across a gigantic number of birds and we’re sure you may at some point lose count of the variety you will see here. After a fun-filled session, you will have your picnic lunch in Tarangire National Park at the best picnic site. Once you are done with that, our driver or guide will take you to the accommodation where you will call it a day and eventually have dinner and overnight.Dinner and Overnight will be at Chem Chem Safari Lodge.

Accommodation: Chem Chem Safari Lodge

DAY 3: TRANSIT TO NDUTU AREA

Morning after having breakfast you will be picked up by our driver guide to Ndutu Area for a game drive. Stunned by the previous days spotting of the migration, today you get another chance to view the beautiful wildlife spectacle. The Ndutu region is a part of the glorious Serengeti National Park as well as the Ngorongoro Conservation Area (NCA). Today you will be following the migration depending on its location in either NCA or Serengeti. During game drives at the right season between December and April, you will be able to see the fertile plains turn into calving grounds and nursery for the great migration. This season attracts hordes of predators prying on vulnerable young wildebeest. Watching the circle of life take its course will be an unforgettable experience etched for life.You will now head towards your accommodation, where a freshly prepared dinner awaits you. Ensure you get some good rest and recharge yourself for the coming day.A luxury camp situated on the banks of lake Masek in Ndutu and located between the enormous plains of Serengeti and the north eastern part of the Ngorongoro Conservation Area.

Accommodation: Lake Masek Tented Lodge

DAY 4: NDUTU AREA –SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

The Ndutu region, situated in the Southern Serengeti and Ngorongoro conservation area, a sublime landscape of the southern Serengeti eco-system. The Ndutu calving season is pretty special! Usually, around December each year the herds start congregating in the area with the peak of the calving season being from January to March. In the backdrop, you’ll spot zebra and gazelle grazing in the sprawling grasslands too. Lake Ndutu, alkaline like most of the other Rift Valley Lakes, is however a viable source of water and is frequented by a wide array of the myriad wildlife here inhabiting. The majority of the wildebeest migration can normally be found on the short-grass plains from December to April. The area is usually heavily populated with elephant, giraffe, over four hundred species of birds and a plethora of other resident game. Afternoon you will return to the accommodation for hot lunch meal, resting after lunch. Late afternoon you will be taken to Central Serengeti National Park with en-routing game drive.The Serengeti National Park is arguably the most impressive wildlife sanctuary in the world. You will have a late afternoon game drive in the endless plains of the Serengeti for a chance to see the great herds of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles or a pride of lions lounging in the shade.The eventful day comes to an end with a heavy dinner and a good night’s rest in your luxury tented camp. Dinner and overnight will be at Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti in Central Serengeti.

Accommodation: Four Seasons Safari Lodge

DAY 5: CENTRAL SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

Early after having a delicious and heavy breakfast, depart for a game drive you will leave our accommodation in Serengeti with a game drive to explore the central portion of the Serengeti National Park today. The legendary Serengeti National Park is the most renowned safari destination for its incredible population of lions and leopards. The central portion of the Serengeti – known as Seronera area is one of the richest wildlife habitats in the park. It features the Seronera River, which provides a valuable water source to this area and therefore attracts wildlife well representative of most of the Serengeti’s species. During the off season of the migration, one must really look out for the many lion pride that thrive in this region. The interactions and survival of these lion pride provide an excellent game viewing experience all year round. Along with a picnic lunch during the full day game drive evening. We are completely flexible with your preferences and this day will be doing another full game drive since the Serengeti is large and vast we can find different areas to exploit and spot animals and when you are lucky enough you can even see some events such as animal hunting and fighting. The eventful day comes to an end with a heavy dinner and a good night’s rest in your accommodation. Overnight at Serengeti in Central Serengeti.The eventful day comes to an end with a heavy dinner and a good night’s rest in your luxury tented camp. Dinner and overnight will be at Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti in Central Serengeti.

Accommodation: Four Seasons Safari Lodge

DAY 6: CENTRAL SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

Depart this morning for a game drive in the central part of the Serengeti. This part is famous for the abundance of lions, Cheetahs, Elephants and leopards that can usually be seen quite easily.Game viewing in this central area is spectacular all year round. Enjoy the views of endless plains and stunning wildlife like cheetahs, elephants, giraffes, hippos, ostrich, aardvark, pangolin and many more. This day will be little relaxed day we can have a morning game drive before going back to the lodge for lunch and evening taken to evening game drive.Overnight at Serengeti in Central Serengeti.The eventful day comes to an end with a heavy dinner and a good night’s rest in your luxury tented camp. Dinner and overnight will be at Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti in Central Serengeti.

Accommodation: Four Seasons Safari Lodge

DAY 7: CENTRAL SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

Starting your day game drive after a filling breakfast, you will explore the central portion of the Serengeti National Park today. The legendary Serengeti National Park is the most renowned safari destination for its incredible population of lions and leopards. The central portion of the Serengeti – known as Seronera area is one of the richest wildlife habitats in the park. It features the Seronera River, which provides a valuable water source to this area and therefore attracts wildlife well representative of most of the Serengeti’s species. During the off season of the migration, one must really look out for the many lion prides that thrive in this region. The interactions and survival of these lion prides provide an excellent game viewing experience all year round. Afternoon returning to accommodation for lunch before packing your things and then start an en-routing game drive/transfer to Ngorongoro for dinner and overnight. The long day comes to an end with a heavy dinner and a good night’s rest in your accommodation. Get a good nap, for the following day you will move towards the Ngorongoro Crater.The Highlands has redefined the Ngorongoro Crater experience, offering an array of activities afforded by the camp’s exclusive location and a combination of comfort and contemporary style.The Highlands perches at the edge of a forest that encircles Olmoti Volcano. It is the highest property in the area, affording sublime, panoramic vistas. Both sunrise and sunset are visible from camp.

Accommodation: The Highlands Ngorongoro

DAY 8: NGORONGORO CRATER– (NGORONGORO CONSERVATION AREA)

You will wake up early morning, having your breakfast in the morning before starting to depart to Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive, the 600 m deep caldera you will descend the crater for a game drive. The Ngorongoro Crater is the world’s largest inactive, intact and unfilled volcanic caldera. It has a massive floor of about 260 sq. kms with a depth of over 2000 feet. Ngoro Ngoro Crater is the largest collapsed volcanic crater in the world and fourteen kilometers of isolated natural beauty. Ngoro Ngoro Crater is surrounded by a ring of extinct volcanoes and the floor, dotted with watering holes, shelters some 30,000 animals. The 5-6 hour game drive on the crater floor will show you a lot of animal action. Keeping the camera ready is definitely recommended. Since Ngorongoro Crater is compact you can have an access to see different and many animals and birds in short period of time. The African elephant, buffalo, Black rhino, Hippos, Hyenas, Cheetahs and Lions are found in plenty.Afternoon you will begin a steep ascend to the top exit of the crater to the accommodation for lunch. With an incredible experience and loads of memories to cherish, dinner and overnight.

Accommodation: The Highlands Ngorongoro

DAY 9: LAKE EYASI TO THE BUSHMEN

Morning after waking up you will have your breakfast in your accommodation , This day will be full of culture,in this Amazing Honeymoon Holiday will be cultural experience with interesting encounters with local tribes. This is an untouched and scenically beautiful area where you can get a real insight into the way of life of the rural tribes like the Datoga and Hadzabe Bushmen. The Datoga are farmers and silversmith’s who make iron knives and spears. The Hadzabe hunt on wild animals with bow and arrows, eating roots, honey and wild fruits. Visit their habitat and experience their culture first hand. Later afternoon you will be taken to Lake Manyara Serena Lodge for afternoon lunch and hotel relaxation, Dinner and overnight will be provided.Overnight will be at Chem Chem Safari Lodge.

DAY 10: LAKE MANYARA NATIONAL PARK – ZANZIBAR ISLAND

After morning breakfast our driver guide will be waiting for you at your accommodation ready for starting your safari to Lake Manyara National Park. This park is one of Tanzania’s small National Parks and it is placed the most dramatically wildlife area in Tanzania. Also this Park consists of a huge shallow salt Lake covering which covers about two-third of the park and lies at the base of the Great Rift Valley’s Due to rich and varied habitats, this park attracts different animal species like herds of zebra, giraffe, warthog, wildebeest, buffalo, African elephant, the famous Lake Manyara tree-climbing lions etc. Countless numbers of flamingos attracted by green algae around the Lake are also available. This park is smallest and very compact, this will help you to see many animal species, bird species, trees and the beautiful Lake of Manyara, afternoon you will be taken back to the accommodation at Lake Manyara Serena Lodge for Lunch, then you will take your things ,basing on your flight time you will be taken to Arusha Airstrip ready to catch your flight to Zanzibar .Upon your arrival in Zanzibar Island you will be met our representative from Signature Safari who will take you to the Hotel for relaxation and resting and enjoying beach view.Upon your arrival in Zanzibar, you will be picked up by our professional guide and transferred to Madinat Al Bahr Hotel .Offering direct access to a white-sand beach along the Indian Ocean, this luxury hotel is 4 km from Abeid Amani Karume International Airport, and 8 km from both the People’s Palace Museum and Forodhani Park.

Accommodation: Madinat Al Bahr Hotel

DAY 11: ZANZIBAR ISLAND – STONE TOWN / SPICE TOUR

After breakfast, today you will have chance to explore the stone town tour which is the most scenic parts of the island as you head to Stone Town. In stone town you will experience the rich history of the city, after that we head to the bustling markets, alleys and what was formerly a slave market. In the afternoon, we proceed with tour of the countryside to the spice plantations where you will be welcomed by the rich aroma of spices such as nutmeg and cinnamon. Spices and herbs were originally introduced to Zanzibar by Portuguese traders in the 16th century, brought from their colonies in South America and India. During this tour you will be walking inside of spice farms in Swahili we call them (Shambas) While you are there, you shall see how the spices, herbs and fruits grow and are cultivated as well as more explanations from your guide about how those crops are used in our daily life. You will be smelling and tasting spices, herbs and tropical fruits such as clove, lemongrass, nutmeg, cinnamon, turmeric, vanilla, coconuts, papaya, chili, black pepper, jack-fruit, cardamom, cassava and oranges. After the tour you will enjoy a traditional Swahili lunch at the farm. Here is when you can really taste the different ways of using spices. The tour finishes with a stop at a spice stall selling fresh packaged spices (cash only). In the evening you will be driven to Hotel in Zanzibar for dinner and overnight.Overnight will be at Madinat Al Bahr Hotel .Offering direct access to a white-sand beach along the Indian Ocean.

Accommodation: Madinat Al Bahr Hotel

DAY 12: ZANZIBAR ISLAND – ROCK RESTAURANT

On this day you will be taken to The Rock Restaurant in Zanzibar spend the day here having lunch and then later you will be returned to the Hotel for relaxation and your personal activities.Overnight will be at Madinat Al Bahr Hotel .Offering direct access to a white-sand beach along the Indian Ocean.

Accommodation: Madinat Al Bahr Hotel

DAY 13: ZANZIBAR ISLAND – PRISON ISLAND & NAKUPENDA BEACH

You will be met by our professional guide at 9:00 am for prison island tour where by you will enjoy the swing the Jiang tortoise, peacocks and small antelopes known as dikki dikki, after Prison Island you will later visit Nakupenda Beach. This full day tour includes something for everyone! Start your day by visiting the historical Prison Island. There, tour a prison once used as a quarantine, befriend an Aldabra Tortoise and enjoy the panorama of Stone town viewed from an island! After that, it’s time to relax on the white sands of the Pange Sandbank and soak up the Zanzibar sun. Admire the breathtaking view of the turquoise waters that surround you and the birds that loom up above. This truly is a dream destination! Time for a cool down? Grab your snorkel and flippers and go for a swim. This underwater world will blow you away. Marvel at the tropical fish that swim amongst the beautiful, live coral reef that surrounds the Sandbank. After you’ve worked up an appetite, sit down and relax under your own private tent, where a seafood feast awaits you. Sure to be one of your favorite days in Zanzibar! Nakupenda beach, tour Zanzibar iconic whitest sandbank. The beach boasts of fabulous sandbanks and a beautiful coastline, ideal for snorkeling, swimming & sun bathing. You will also chance to visit prison island see, touch, and feed and take pictures of the Aldabra Giant Tortoise at Prison Island. In the evening you will be driven to Hotel in Zanzibar for Overnight.Overnight will be at Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa.

Accommodation: Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

DAY 14: ZANZIBAR ISLAND – ROCK RESTAURANT

On this day you will be taken to The Rock Restaurant in Zanzibar spend the day and Amazing Honeymoon Holiday here having lunch and then later you will be returned to the Hotel for relaxation and your personal activities.Overnight will be at Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa.
