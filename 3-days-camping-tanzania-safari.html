03 Days Tanzania Camping Safari Highlights

This 3 Days Camping Tanzania Safari Manyara,Ngorongoro and Tarangire tour will take you to the most visited destination with high wildlife concentration and amazing sceneries. This place is characterized by the large herds of elephants, tree climbing lions, and the fascinating baobab trees, some 20,000 mammals live including about 20 black rhinoceros, making this three parks to be among of very best place in Tanzania to see these amazing creatures.

Day 0: Arrival Day

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be met by your driver and transferred Karibu Heritage House in Arusha for dinner and overnight. Settle into your accommodation, ready to start your safari proper in the morning. If time permits, a complimentary town tour can be arranged.

Accommodation: Karibu Heritage House

After breakfast, you will start your first day of the 3 Days Camping Tanzania Safari for a drive to Tarangire National Park.Our personal safari guide and driver will meet you at hotel to undertake the drive, on good tarmac road, the some hundred and twenty kilometers to Tarangire National Park where we undertake our first day of game drives. Tarangire well known for its elephant populations, three families numbering in the region of one thousand each, is so named for the life giving Tarangire River which wends its way through the park and gives sustenance from its cooling waters to a broad variety of game that come here to imbibe. We may hope to see zebra, wildebeest, buffalo, elephant, and giraffe, to name a few. It is possible we will glimpse our friend leo panthera, simba, the mighty lion, stalking his prey or, the leopard watching in relaxation in the trees above. An ornithological paradise, some five hundred birds have been here identified. Returning to our lodge for a hot lunch we again at 1400 hrs head out to continue our game viewing experience in the park, i search of the creatures at their daily business With the day coming to a close and the sun setting over The Rift Valley our driver will convey us to our accommodation for a delectable dinner and an overnights .Kizumba Campsite is located at the top of East African rift valley ,it gives you a great and amazing view of beautiful Lake Manyara.

Accommodation: Kizumba Public Campsite

Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

After an early breakfast, depart with picnic lunch and drive to Ngorongoro crater for a full day of game drive. The crater has a good number of lions and cheetahs, and it is another good place to see the rare black rhino.Finishing up with a quick breakfast leave Kizumba to Ngorongoro Conservation Area,you will make an early descent in the norning into the crater floor. The Ngorongoro Crater is the worldâ€™s largest inactive, intact and unfilled volcanic caldera. It has a massive floor of about 260 sq. kms with a depth of over 2000 feet. The 5-hour game drive on the crater floor will show you a lot of animal action. Keeping the camera ready is definitely recommended. The African elephant, buffalo, Black rhino, Hippos, Hyenas, Cheetahs and Lions are found in plenty. Post the picnic lunch at the beautiful Hippo pool, you will begin a steep ascend to the top exit of the crater. This is the last leg of your safari, with a 4hr drive left to Arusha. You will be dropped off to your preferred location in Arusha by 6:00 PM in the evening. With an incredible experience and loads of memories to cherish, this is the time you bid goodbye to your team Ngorongoro Crater is the largest collapsed volcanic crater in the world and fourteen kilometers of isolated natural beauty. Ngorongoro Crater is surrounded by a ring of extinct volcanoes and the floor, dotted with watering holes, shelters some 30,000 animals. The eventful day comes to an end with a departure to the top of rift valley at Kizumba Campsite for Dinner, Overnight and Breakfast.

Accommodation: Kizumba Public Campsite
