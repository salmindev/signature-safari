07 Days Perfect Family Safari in Tanzania

Signature Safari 7 Day Perfect Family Safari in Tanzania offers you the opportunity to avail of the finest of luxury accommodation at the incredible discount of 25% in April and July of 2023-25. We will on this adventure take in some of the finest parks that the Northern Circuit has to offer. The Five Star Lodges will see us accommodated in the lap of luxury and our Signature Safari driver and guide will with intimate knowledge ensure we get the most out of our safari experience. On this trip we visit Tarangire National Park, Manyara National Park, The Serengeti National Park, And The Ngorongoro Conservation Area. With the rains coming at this time of year, April/May, the wildlife and landscapes experience a rejuvenation with great activity from the creatures here inhabiting. We are guaranteed game drives choc full of animal life in this beautiful 7 Days Perfect Family Safari in Tanzania!

Day 0: Arrival Day

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be met by your driver and transferred to the hotel in Arusha for relaxation ,dinner and overnight. Briefing will be provided.Settle into your accommodation, ready to start your safari in the morning.

Accommodation: Arusha Kibo Palace Hotel

Day 1: Arusha to Tarangire National Park.

In the morning our Signature Safari driver and guide will at 0800 hrs pick you up from your accommodation for the drive to Tarangire National Park (approximately 2 hours) on well paved roads. Tarangire, best known for its elephant families, savannah, iconic baobab trees, and seasonal swamps, is so named for the life-giving Tarangire River. Animals gather at the river with the park being home to among the myriad creatures giraffe, bushbuck, hartebeest, buffalo, zebra, and wildebeest, all of whom are closely attended by a range of predators, leo panthera the lion, and his elusive cousin the leopard. From the open roof of our state of the art Safari Truck we will be able to absorb the landscape and watch the wildlife. We enjoy a picnic lunch in the park, which includes a delicious varied repast, coffee, and soft drinks. After a full day soaking up the sights of the park, we drive to our carefully selected by us for quality luxury lodgings where we take the opportunity to relax, enjoy dinner in aesthetic surrounds, and retire to our well appointed quarters when the spirit moves us.We will thereafter head onwards to our accommodation at Tarangire Lake Burunge Tented Lodge

Accommodation: Lake Burunge Tented Lodge Tarangire

Day 2: Tarangire National Park to Manyara National Park.

Rise and shine, we make a very early start today to enjoy further game drives in the Tarangire National Park. We return to our lodge at 0800 hrs to enjoy a hearty breakfast before departing for our drive to Manyara National Park enjoying on arrival a hot lunch in our luxury lodge. At 1400 hrs we set off on our game drives. Manyara, a relatively small but diverse park 120km west of Arusha, is so named for the shallow salt lake that covers some seventy percent of the surface area, flooding and drying with the seasons and home to thousands of flamingo and in the extraordinary region of 500 other bird species. On our exploration of the park we will endeavour to see baboon, blue and vervet monkey, warthog, giraffe, zebra, wildebeest, buffalo, elephant and, with some luck, lions lounging in the trees. The park is known for its diverse landscapes; not only open grassy plains, but also primate-filled woodlands and baobab dotted cliffs. At about 1700 hrs we make our way to our luxury lodge for a delectable dinner and relaxation in the sublime surrounds of our carefully chosen lodgings with our excellent quarters on hand to harbour our rest and dreams of wild cats!

Accommodation: Lake Manyara Serena Safari Lodge

Day 3: En-route Gamedrive to Serengeti National Park

We make an early start today to make the most of our adventure and, after our breakfast, at 0730 hrs we set out on our adventure to the one of the seven wonders of Africa, The Serengeti National Park, taking our lunch on arrival at our lodge at 1200 hrs before, at 1400 hrs, heading out for our afternoon game drive. The Serengeti, largest park in Africa at 15,000 sq. km, is home to some three million large mammals and is made up of a diversity of landscapes with the southern region being dominated by the lush grassy plains that draw the two million wildebeest, half a million zebra and some quarter million antelope to make the hazardous journey in search pasture when the autumn rains come. All of ‘the big five’ here inhabit, elephant, lion, buffalo, rhino, and the elusive leopard, alongside a multitude of other mammals. Grant’s and Thompson’s gazelle, cheetah, hyena, eland, hartebeest, bushbuck, the flightless ostrich, marabou stork, and the largest flighted bird in the world, the kory bustard. We seek out these creatures with the keen intuition and experience of our Signature guide and, with day drawing to a close, we wend our way to our selected lodgings, The to unwind, dine and take our ease by the fire under the endless East African sky.You will be picked up to the Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge , where a delectation of dining, rest and, relaxation await us, and a sleep upon the ‘Endless Plain’ as our minds eye roves over the sights and sounds of the day.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge

Day 4: Full day Serengeti National Park

After our heavy breakfast, we will start our Perfect Family Safari in Tanzania as we head out to enjoy a full day game drive on the Serengeti Plains. We will endeavour to explore the range of different habitats found here that include swamps, woodland, soda lakes, and the world famous Serengeti short grass plains and encounter some of the myriad wildlife that call this landscape home. With our knowledgeable guide leading us out upon the plain using unerring instinct we can hope to come into contact with a broad range of creatures on our days game drive. Breaking for a hot lunch at the lodge we continue into the evening taking in the awesome spectacle of this world renowned landscape and enjoying the experience of coming into contact with the wildlife of wild East Africa. with evening drawing in and shadows beginning to cloak the plains we wend our way back to our luxury lodge for delectation in dining, relaxation, and a going over in the minds eye the sights and sounds of the day.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge

Day 5: Serengeti - Ngorongoro Conservation Area (Walking Tour).

We will start our Perfect Family Safari in Tanzania after our breakfast at the lodge we set out at 0800-0900 hrs for our drive up into the Ngorongoro highlands where we will stop at the crater rim to take in the stunning view of this, the largest Caldera on planet Earth. With our luxury lodge sitting up on the lip of this 610 metre deep crater we take our lunch whilst soaking up the awesome view. Formed in the massive explosion of a volcano some two to three million years ago that once rivalled Kilimanjaro in height, this enclosed eco system is home to some twenty five thousand large mammals including all of ‘the big five’ with an estimated fifty of the endangered Northern Black Rhino calling this landscape their home. At 1500 hrs we set out for a walking safari in the Conservation area with an armed ranger and our guide accompanying us and giving us further information about the sublime landscape which we find ourselves traversing. We have the option of making a visit to a Maasai village, later on we will be taken to dine in ease and comfort at our splendid partner lodge,Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge taking in the view and preparing for our rest in anticipation of our adventure to come.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge

Day 6: Full Day Ngorongoro Crater Tour.

Rise and shine, today we visit one of the most extraordinary landscapes. Again we take an early breakfast so as to enjoy our adventure to the maximum .After early breakfast we descend the windy bumpy track to the crater floor. More precisely a caldera, this 600 metre deep 304 square kilometer enclosed ecosphere offers the best game viewing opportunities in all of Tanzania. With some 25,000 large mammals within the caldera, as soon as we hit the floor, we will have the opportunity to see in addition to ‘the big five’ elephant, lion, rhino, buffalo, and leopard, the various springs attract animals such as wildebeest, gazelle, reedbuck, bushbuck, hartebeest, hippo, antelope, both Grant’s and Thompson’s gazelle, impala, to name a few, as well as over 500 species of bird. It is well recognised as one of the world’s wonders, veritably a magnificent spectacle for the human witness lucky enough to traverse her wildlife laden floor. Lunch served at a picnic site by the hippo pool followed by further game drives with at 1530, our ascent once more to the Crater Highlands and our delectation in dining in an aesthetically excellent restaurant with our quarters awaiting to harbour our rest under the starry endless African sky to complete our safari ,Dine in ease and comfort at our splendid partner lodge,Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge taking in the view and preparing for our rest in anticipation of our adventure to come.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge

Day 7: Ngorongoro – Depart.

After morning breakfast you will have some time to relax at the hotel before hotel -checkout and our driver will take you to the Arusha to complete our tour or basing on your flight schedule you will be picked up to the Kilimanjaro International Airport or Arusha Airport ready to catch your flight to your next destination.
