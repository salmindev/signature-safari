05 Days Masai Mara Great Migration Safari

Have you ever watched the movie, Lion King?Well, imagine that coming to life and being a part of it. This 5 Days Migration Safaris is not your average african holiday. People all over the world come to Kenya to witness this grand spectacle. Indeed, an exprience that will live on forever, a story you can tell that just earns more value everytime you share it. The migration is an ongoing movement of animals that takes place throughout the year. The migration occurs within an area that is known as the “Serengeti ecosystem.” The 40,000-square mile area is defined by Masai Mara National Reserve in the north and, in the south, Ngorongoro Conservation Area and Maswa Game Reserve in Tanzania. The migration is not a singular, isolated event. Instead, the phrase describes the constant movement of over 1.5 million wildebeests and hundreds of thousands of zebras, as well as elands and gazelles. As with other wildlife, the purpose of the movement is the search for pasture and water.

Day 1: Arrival - Masai Mara National Reserve

Arrival in Nairobi International Airport. Once you have disembarked from your international flight you’ll be welcomed by signature safari guide and he will drive you to the lodge for dinner and overnight Lotos Inn and Suites which offers fully furnished, luxurious serviced suites located in the heart of Nairobi’s upmarket Westlands area.

Accommodation: Lotos Inn and Suites

Day 2- 4: Masai Mara National Reserve 2 nights

Over the next three days of 5 Days Great Migration Safaris you’ll enjoy morning and afternoon game drives in the Mara’s riverine woodlands and on the open plains. As well as game drives, there are many other exciting activities that you can enjoy during your time in the Masai Mara – including horse riding, black rhino tracking experiences, cultural visits to Maasai villages and markets etc. There’s also the option to experience a magical early morning hot air balloon flight over the plains and forests of the Masai Mara followed by a champagne breakfast at your landing point. All meals and overnight will be at Kichwa Tembo Camp maasai Mara.

Accommodation: Kichwa Tembo Camp

Day 5: Nairobi to Departure

This morning you’ll have time to enjoy one final safari activity in the Masai Mara, before returning to your lodge or camp for lunch. This afternoon you’ll be driven back to the airstrip for your light aircraft flight to Nairobi.Most international flights depart from Nairobi in the evening and to ensure that you don’t waste the final day of your holiday waiting at the airport we can arrange for you to have a car and driver for the afternoon and you can visit places of interest in Nairobi.We recommend visiting the Giraffe Centre in Lang’ata or the Karen Blixen Museum, or if you want to buy some souvenirs you can head to Utamaduni, the Maasai market at Galleria Mall. In the evening your driver will transfer you to Nairobi International Airport in plenty time to check in for your return flight home.
