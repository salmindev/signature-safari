Tanzania Budget Camping Safaris

During these Safari’s you will be camping out every night, not only does this keep the cost low for your Safari but you are as close to nature as you can be. Signature Safaris offers Tanzania Budget Camping Safari in Tanzanian National Park and Mountain as well as luxury & budget lodges to the most premier Tanzania wildlife areas. Apart from visiting National Parks, Game Reserves, Open areas in wild Africa, few of our camping safari adventures can take you to the Cultural Sites where local people who live by depending on gathering and hunting dwel. You will be staying on campsites within the Tanzanian National Parks, you will still have your amenities close by but will be living the wildlife. Camping safaris Tanzania are more popular on Northern Tanzania than in the Southern. The Safaris in Northern Circuit includes Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Lake Manyara and Tarangire National Parks as well as Arusha and Kilimanjaro National Parks.While on the Southern circuit we have Selous Game Reserve, Mikumi National Park, Ruaha National Park and Udzungwa Mountain National park. Camping is a great way to really experience living with nature plus it is budget friendly. Especially when compared to Tented Lodge Safari’s or Luxury Lodge Safaris. Many of the travelers who choose to do their Safari with Signature Safari choose to stay in tents in order to be close to nature and to enjoy the Tanzanian National Parks beautiful landscapes and wilderness. When booking accommodation for our wheelchair user clients, we prioritize the following factors to ensure our clients enjoy their stay in Tanzania:

  • Elevators/lift access
  • Wheelchair-accessible public areas
  • Wheelchair-accessible public and private bathrooms
  • Ground-floor rooms with wider doorways
  • Paraplegic-accessible rooms and restaurants
  • Rooms and suites with ramps
  • Bigger tubs with bars and handles
  • Shower seats available
  • Grab bars in shower and tub
  • Hand-held shower nozzle
  • Ramps and handrails
